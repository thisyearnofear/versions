Section 01 — Submission

Submit a version.

A demo, a live take, the cut your label told you to bury. Upload the file and the metadata. The 0.50 USDC submission fee funds the curator pool — split 70/20/10 between the curators who rate your take, the platform, and your own MusicBrainz attribution. Need a wallet? Install Phantom (Solana) to sign submissions; payments can be confirmed in MetaMask when the proxy is wired to real Arc testnet.
